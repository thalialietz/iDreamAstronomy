About

What is iDreamAstronomy?

iDreamAstronomy was made for those who are in love with astronomy and are interested in learning more about all asteroids that orbit Earth.

Why was iDreamAstronomy developed?

iDreamAstronomy was created as a final project for Hackbright Academy's Software Engineering Fellowship program.

Who is the developer behind iDreamAstronomy?

The developer behind this amazing website is Thalia Lietz, an immigrant originally from Brazil, with a background in HR, who decided to become a Software Engineer recently after years of loving technology. Thalia Lietz had an idea to start iDreamAstronomy as her final project after her love for Astronomy.

Where does all the information comes from?

All the information display throughout the website comes from NASA Open API's named Asteroids - NeoWs and Apod, all the pictures and videos comes from Nasa's Image and Video Library.
