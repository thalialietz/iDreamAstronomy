About

What is iDreamAstronomy for?

On iDreamAstronomy you can view Near Earth Asteroids, see the Astronomy Picture of the Day, most known as APOD, and see APOD pictures from past days. You can choose asteroids that get your attention and add them to your journal so that you can easily find them later.

Why was iDreamAstronomy developed?

iDreamAstronomy was created as a final project for Hackbright Academy's Software Engineering Fellowship program.

Who is the developer behind iDreamAstronomy?

The developer behind iDreamAstronomy is Thalia Lietz. She is an immigrant originally from Brazil with a background in HR who decided to become a Software Engineer. Thalia Lietz had an idea to start iDreamAstronomy as her final project stemming from her love of Astronomy.

Where does all the information comes from?

All the information displayed throughout the website comes from NASA Open API's named Asteroids - NeoWs and APOD, all the pictures and videos come from Nasa's Image and Video Library.
