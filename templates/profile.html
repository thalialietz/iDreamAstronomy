If this is your first time or if you forgot about something please see below for the tutorials on how to navigate throughout the website.

Each page on this website also contains an education video for your viewing pleasure.

All Asteroids Page

To see all asteroids, click on the Asteroids button on the navbar. You can select dates within a week from the start date. Once you click on submit you will be taken to a page with all the asteroids for that selected time.

See details for a asteroid

To see details about a specific asteroid, click on its name and you will be taken to a description page.

My Journal Page

To see "My Journal" page, click on the navbar "My Journal" and you will be redirected to your page with all currently added asteroids.

Add an asteroid to your journal

To save an asteroid for later viewing, simply click on the button "Save to your journal" and you will be redirected to your "My Journal" page where you can see the asteroid just added.

Remove an asteroid from your journal

To remove an asteroid from your journal, simply click on the button "Remove from journal".

APOD - Astronomy Picture of the day

To see the astronomy picture of the day, simply click on the navbar. The picture you will see is the current date's picture. To see pictures from the past select a date on the calendar and click Submit. If it's a interactive image you will see a link to click that will redirect you to it.

Account Settings

By clicking on "My Account" on top right, you can choose to change your password, change information on the account, or log out.
